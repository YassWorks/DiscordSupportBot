## what is it about?

AINS (Artificial Intelligence National Summit) is an annual summit hosted by the IEEE Computer Society Chapter at INSAT. It gathers AI and data science enthusiasts for workshops, talks, competitions, a poster session, and a hackathon. It aims to democratize intelligence and inspire innovation within the Tunisian and regional tech community.

## when is it held?

Usually in late June.

## what are the technical fields involved?

- Artificial intelligence
- Data science
- Machine learning
